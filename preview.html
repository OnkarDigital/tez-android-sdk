Behaviour Reference  ·  v1.0.9

QR "I've Paid" button FIX Tap to directly start status polling without leaving the app. Fixes QR payments where onResume never fired.
Amount card NEW Purple-tinted card showing "Amount to Pay" label + bold ₹ value. Color tints with headerColor.
OR SCAN QR divider NEW Horizontal rule with text, shown only when show_qr=true and qr_image is not blank.
QR card NEW White rounded card (tez_qr_card_bg) with border + subtle shadow. Replaces plain centered QR image.
Powered by TezGateway Logo image from tezgateway.com/logo.png. Hidden when remove_branding = true.
Spinner logo Shield logo centered inside spinner ring. Loaded from tezgateway.com/common/img/logoshild.png.
Header color From CheckoutSettings.headerColor. Also applied to spinner tint, "Check Status" border, "I've Paid" button.
Google Pay button Shares QR image via ACTION_SEND + FileProvider. Falls back to gpay_link if no QR.
Status polling Polls every 5s for up to 100s (20 attempts). Stops immediately on SUCCESS or FAILURE.
Result cards Success (green) auto-dismisses 1.8s, Failure (red) 2s, Pending (amber) stays until user closes.
News ticker Marquee at bottom, shown only when settings.news is not blank.
